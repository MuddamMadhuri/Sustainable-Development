Welcome to LPU's Water Conservation Campaign!

  • Water is a precious resource that sustains life on earth, yet it's finite and vulnerable to depletion.
  • As a responsible institution, we are committed to conserving water and promoting sustainable practices to ensure a better future for generations to come.
Campaign Objectives
  1. Raise Awareness: Educate our university community about the importance of water conservation and impact of our daily actions on waater resources.
  2. Reduce Consumption: Encourage students, faculty, and staff to adopt water-saving habits and practices in their daily lives.
  3. Implement Solutions:Colaborate with campus facilities and organizations to implement water-saving technologies and infrastructure.

This campaign will be held on 5thMarch, 2024 in unipolis timings will be from 10am to 1pm.
